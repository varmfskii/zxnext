Spectdrum/Convox

This is 8-bit D/A audio. It is enabled by setting bit 3 of Next register $08 (8). After that audio can be controlled by writing linear 8-bit unsigned values to port $df (223).

Code: 

;; enable SpecDrum/Convox audio
ld bc,$243B
ld a,$08
out (c),a
ld bc,$253B
in a,(c)
or $08
out (c),a
